Coding Buddies

Founder

A couple of friends and I, decided to start an organization called Coding Buddies. We realized that when we were younger, we had no idea what computer science was. Contrary to that, we were all considering university programs that involved computer science. As a result, we founded this organization to give youth a headstart in coding especially since STEM is so prevalent today. The idea was to give students a basic understanding of what coding is using the Python language. We gathered a group of mentors that helped us along the way, leading to a successful first year of operation. The organization still runs to this day.
